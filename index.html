About Me

I am Shreya, the girl you'll always find laughing around the corner. Yes, that's right, people often describe me this way, and I have to admit, it's pretty accurate! I am a person who lives in the moment and enjoys every bit of what comes my way. I love to express myself through different media, push beyond my comfort zone and explore the myriad of opportunities this world holds.

Since the age of 5, I have been exploring my artistic self. I am professionally trained in Ballet, which has made me a versatile dancer able to do Contemprory, Indian Classical and Folk dance. Dance has challenged me at every stage and has made me stronger, both physically and mentally.

I also express myself through the medium of art, something I engage in as a hobby. Nothing is better to clear my mind or take a break than just being able to pen down my thoughts on a piece of canvas. Paintings and sketches provide a very soothing outlet for my thoughts.

Lately, I have been expressing my ideas though the web and have been actively engaging in web design. This is one such project, which contains links to my other works. I hope you like them.
