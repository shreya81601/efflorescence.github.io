Web-Design Projects

My journey in Web-designing began with me building 4 websites, showcasing different usages of the web and the way we can communicate with the world through it.

"1. Bodacious Black"

A Company which specializes in Black Clothing lines for Men and Women of all age groups.

This was my first website which is built around a video from NYUAD's IM CommLab class as a part of the 30 Minute Film Festival (30MFF). The cast in the video can be seen throwing black clothes around and having a lot of fun, hence a black clothing website which uses that as its hype/promo video.

I personally love wearing black clothes and inspired by the cheerful, happy vibe of the video, I went for a playful, yet subtle theme for my website. Black is the master of colors, especially in terms of clothing bacause it goes with every attire and is suitable for any ocassion. It has a certain boldness to it, hence the title 'Bodacious Black'.

I tried to bring out this characteristic in my web page and designed it specifically to suit the above. The choice of colors, the layout and the pictures strive to create a look of a highend brand, one that is sophisticated, but not too elegant to seem very expensive. I wanted to ensure that my site has an approchable and affordable feel to it.
